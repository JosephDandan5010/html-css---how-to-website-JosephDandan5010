Finding Diamonds in Minecraft

Step 1. Get the right tools!

To find and mine some sweet sweet diamonds you need to first gather the proper tools. A simple wood, stone, or gold pickaxe won't do though you will need an iron pickaxe to mine it with. Once you have the correct pickaxe in hand, a plethora of torches, a trusty weapon, and some durable armor you are ready to start mining for diamonds! But wait how are you going to even find them?

Step 2. Know where to mine!

Diamond Ore only spawns in layers y 1-16, but is most abundantly on y 11-12. To check what y level you are on hit f3. Diamonds can spawn in veins as large as 8 blocks and as small as 1 block. There are some dangers in searching for diamonds mobs, gravel suffication, and most frequently lava. Lava appears most frequent on y 4-10 so to stay a bit safer and make sure your diamonds don't get burnt to a crisp try to stay above those layers.

Step 3. Exploring Caves!

An effective fun method of finding diamonds is exploring cave systems which can sometimes be massive. Diamond Ore can also be pretty easy to spot in those massive cave systems and if its across lava just carry your trusty water bucket to get across.

Step 4. Mining Methods!

There are multiple and effective methods in the art of finding diamonds.

The Staircase Mining Method

Since you should never mine straight down in minecraft the stair case method is a great way of exploring the depths of the world safely while finding a lot of ores along the way.

The Branch Mining Method

A very effective way to find diamonds is to branch mine by making a 2x2 tunnel or more and digging individual branches by however so many blocks. This method covers a lot of ground quickly without having to break any uneccessary blocks.

The Strip Mining Method

Strip mining is a lot less fun then it sounds. It involves mining everything in hopes of receiving a rare ore. You can either strip mine in a singular 1x1 line or you can expand from a box shaped area. Both are time consuming, but you will get a ton of building blocks, resources, and a cool space to build in!

Other Tips!

Other methods of obtaining diamonds are less common for example chests in abandoned mineshafts, villages, dungeons, temples, strongholds, nether fortress's, and end cities all have a chance for a diamond in it, but don't rely on chests in your pursuit of diamonds. Don't get too excited make sure there isn't any lava underneath to turn those diamonds to dust. Bring a lot of torches unless you want to say "Awww Man".

Summary

These methods/tips are useful and are used by a vast majority of the game's playerbase, but if you don't like any of these methods you could always come up with your own to find some of that blue stuff.
